Malaria Epidemiology, Dynamics and Control

4 Malaria Transmission Dynamics

Malaria is complex. The Ross-Macdonald model is a good start, but it scratches the surface. In the following sections, we launch into a set of lessons, we want to present all of malaria from a birds-eye view.
